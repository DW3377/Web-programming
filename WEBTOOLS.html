Web tools refer to software applications that are accessed and used through a web browser. These tools are designed to assist users in performing various tasks, such as communication, collaboration, content creation, and productivity, among others. They are an integral part of the online experience and play a crucial role in simplifying and enhancing digital activities. (What Is a Web Tool?, n.d.)
